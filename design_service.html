Event Design & Florals

Your wedding florals and décor are more than just pretty details — they're the soul of your celebration! They set the mood, tell your story, and create the kind of atmosphere that makes your guests walk in and immediately gasp. From the ceremony arch to the centerpieces to that stunning bridal bouquet, every bloom and every detail matters. And we are HERE for all of it!

Whether you have a crystal clear vision saved across seventeen Pinterest boards or you simply know you want it to feel "romantic and magical" (we speak that language fluently, by the way), our Floral Design & Décor services are here to bring it all to life. We'll work closely with you to create a cohesive, breathtaking aesthetic that feels authentically and beautifully YOU — from the first petal to the last candle.

Here's what's included:

Design & Consultation

  • In-depth style consultation to understand your vision, color palette, and overall aesthetic
  • Creation of a personalized design board to visualize your floral and décor concept
  • Collaborative editing of the design plan until every detail feels just right

Florals

  • Bridal bouquet and bridesmaid bouquets
  • Boutonnieres and corsages for the wedding party
  • Ceremony florals including altar, arch, or backdrop arrangements
  • Aisle décor such as pew flowers, lanterns, or floral markers
  • Cocktail hour and reception centerpieces
  • Head table and sweetheart table floral arrangements
  • Specialty floral installations and statement pieces: stage installations, backdrops

Décor

  • Table linens, napkins, and charger plate coordination
  • Candles, votives, and lighting accents
  • Signage styling — welcome signs, seating charts, menus, and more
  • Place settings and tablescape design
  • Lounge furniture and specialty rental coordination
  • Personal décor items such as card boxes, guest books, and unity candle setups

Day Of

  • Full setup and styling of all floral and décor elements
  • On-site adjustments to ensure everything looks flawless before guests arrive
  • Breakdown and cleanup of all floral and décor items at the end of the night
